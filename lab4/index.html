Simple Task Outline - Changing a Tire

  1. Gather tools/materials
    • Spare Tire
    • Tire iron
    • Jack
    • Tire Wedges
  2. Setup
    • Loosen Lugs
    • Wedge Tires
  3. Lift Car
    1. Locate solid area of frame
    2. Place jack under
    3. lift the car with the jack
  4. Tire Removal
    1. Remove lugs fully with tire iron
    2. Remove Tire
  5. Attach Spare
    1. Mount Spare
    2. Secure spare tire with lugs
  6. Finishing Up
    1. Lower Car with jack
    2. Give lugs a final tightening
    3. Put away tools and materials

Challenge

The challenege of this lab was to approach tasks in a broken down, step by step, form in order to get comfortable with pseudocoding and java script.

Problems

The psuedocoding aspect of this assignment was definitely the most challenging, as it was the newest aspect to me. However, thinking of the code in terms of a simple list broken up into larger and smaller tasks made it much more approachable.

Reflection

One thing this lab made me think about is how much easier something becomes when thought of in terms of a step by step list. This simplifies a lot of the work involved and causes for a much less confusing experience, something that was once overwhelming turned into an approachable and simple process.
